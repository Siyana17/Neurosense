About Neurosense

Neurosense is a device that can sense negative emotions using a Brain Computer Interface. It is a non-invasive device and is safe to use. It can help prevent many severe mental illnesses as well as mild mental illnesses. The BCI can determine the level of your negative emotion. Mild, moderate or severe. It will immediately react based off of the level.

Levels

MILD

When the BCI senses mild negative emotions this means you are feeling pretty down but it hasn't gotten to a dangerous point yet. Once the BCI senses this, a connected AI will automatically speed search google for positive articles, videos, quotes and affirmations. Then you will get a notifaction on your phone from the app. Once you open the app, your feed will be filled with positivity to help you cheer up and remeber good memories.

MODERATE

When the BCI senses moderate negative emotions it means somewhere in the middle. You are feeling down and getting even closer to reaching a bad point but you're not there yet. Once the BCI senses this, it will react with the same process as the mild reaction. If the BCI notices no change you're feed on the app will change to journaling activities and volunteers you can talk to about your feelings to let of the steam.

SEVERE

When the BCI senses severe negative emotions it means that you have reached a dangerously high level of emotion. It's something that needs to be treated immediately or the results will not end well. Once the BCI senses this severe stage, it will react the same way as the mild and moderate stage. But at the same time your negative thoughts will be sent to a therapist. This will help since the therapist will be able to understand what you are really feeling and create a plan/solution to resolve the issue. An AI will also schedule a virtual meeting with the therapist for you
